About

DeftCoders v7.0

We are hoping to give to chances to several teams for each faculity of Uva Wellassa University to participate in that competition. There will be about 250 participants. It will be 12 hours coding session, intended to provide students with an opportunity to demonstrate and improve their coding and problem solving skill.

We are hoping to grant participation certificates for all participants and cash awards for the winners.
